About

Teddy's Auto Sales has been family owned and operated since 1984 selling cars INTERNATIONALLY and LOCALLY!

We would absolutely love to earn your business and trust! One thing that distinguishes our business from the rest, is that we truly bring a unique and comfortable family atmosphere to our dealership. Whether it is our rare collection of antique cars and trucks, our years of experience, or our honest and family orientated way of doing business, I know you will find something to love here at Teddy's Auto Sales! We also are members of the Iowa Automotive Dealers Association and the Hawk A antique club. Once you buy a Automobile from us, you become part of our special and growing family!
